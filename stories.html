TANYA’S STORY

A first person account of how going throught he Uncommon process made an impact on not just one person,but her whole family.

AUTHOR | Tanya DATE | 07/03/2020

It’s cold now in Zimbabwe and I’m wearing a hat with big “USA” letters written on it. I won it in a week long Sudoku challenge, a game I had never played before. It’s something to be proud of, I know, but it almost feels like a painful consolation for larger, missed opportunities.

On my lap is my nine month old baby, Dwayne. I’m reading to him. A book called, ​Quantum Computing for Babies.​ If it l ooks like Dwayne is enjoying the colors, words and shapes in between the covers, don’t be fooled, he really just wants to eat the pages…
