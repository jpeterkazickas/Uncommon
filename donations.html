join the Uncommon Community

Because we pride ourselves on being a new type of startup, we like to think of this community more as investors than donors. By giving monthly, you are investing in the future, and allowing us to plan our budget and expansion plans strategically. Your support is the growth capital we will need to achieve scale, and we are grateful for it!

Want to get your company involved? Become a CSR partner.

We engage our Corporate Social Responsibility partners in many ways - our favorite is the Innovation Hub. When you build and sponsor a hub, you’ll get full branding rights to the space and we’ll prepare annual photo & video content for your company’s marketing purposes. But more than what you’ll get, it’s about the impact you are making! Each Hub means 20 young adults will join our 12-month bootcamp, and 750+ school children will join our after school coding program.

Build a Hub

We’ll work with you to design and build a custom Innovation Hub in one of our high need neighborhoods.

Sponsor a Hub

Sponsoring one of our spaces means covering all of the costs associated with training our young adults & students.

100% Pledge Support

Make a donation dedicated to our operating expenses. This crucial gift allows us to continue to send 100% of any public donation directly to the kids!
