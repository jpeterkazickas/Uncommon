Our Innovation Hubs

It is extremely important to us that our Innovation Hubs are located in the communities we serve. We take great pride in designing and building beautiful, state of the art learning environments that are run by and for the community members.

Rather than taking young adults out of their neighborhoods, we believe in investing in localities with the goal of making long term, sustainble impact. Each of our hubs is unique, and we rely completely on solar power to operate our spaces.
